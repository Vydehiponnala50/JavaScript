A font is a specific style of text, encompassing design, size, and other variations within a typeface. It's the "how" of displaying text, while the typeface is the overall design. For example, Times New Roman is a typeface, but Times New Roman bold 12pt is a font. Fonts are used to convey information, create visual hierarchy, and influence the reader's perception
